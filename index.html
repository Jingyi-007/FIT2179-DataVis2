What Does This Tell Us?

Access to schools is high.
But equity across states
still needs attention.

Malaysia has achieved near-universal literacy supported by a strong public education system. However, disparities between states — particularly between urban centres like Selangor and rural states like Sarawak — suggest that access alone does not guarantee equal outcomes.
